About Us

UA3Innovations is a cutting-edge technology company focused on creating smart solutions for modern problems. Founded by four visionary students from TED University, our mission is to optimize public transportation systems using AI, machine learning, and data-driven methodologies.

Our team is passionate about innovation, and we aim to create sustainable and impactful solutions that benefit communities around the globe. With our expertise in embedded systems, software development, and artificial intelligence, we are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
